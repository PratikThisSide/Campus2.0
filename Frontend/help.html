Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a maintenance request?

To submit a maintenance request:

  1. Navigate to the "Create New Request" section in your dashboard
  2. Select the appropriate category for your issue
  3. Fill out the request form with details about the problem
  4. Add any relevant photos or documents
  5. Click "Submit Request"

You'll receive a confirmation email with your request number.

How long does it take to resolve a request?

Response times vary based on the urgency and type of request:

  • Emergency issues (24/7): Within 2 hours
  • High priority: 24-48 hours
  • Standard requests: 3-5 business days
  • Planned maintenance: Scheduled in advance
Can I track the status of my request?

Yes, you can track your requests in several ways:

  • View the "My Requests" section in your dashboard
  • Check email notifications for status updates
  • Use the mobile app to receive push notifications
  • Contact the maintenance office during business hours
What should I do in case of an emergency?

For emergencies (flooding, electrical hazards, etc.):

  1. Call Campus Security immediately at X5555
  2. Submit an emergency request through the system
  3. If safe to do so, take photos of the issue
  4. Evacuate the area if necessary

Do not attempt to fix dangerous situations yourself.
